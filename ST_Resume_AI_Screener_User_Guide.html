▸ 产品概述Product Overview

ST Resume AI Screener 是意法半导体内部 HR 团队专属的 AI 简历筛选工具。通过大语言模型（Gemini / DeepSeek）对候选人的简历进行多维度自动评分，帮助 HR 快速定位高质量候选人，将筛选效率提升 80% 以上。 ST Resume AI Screener is an AI-powered resume screening tool built for STMicroelectronics HR teams. It leverages LLMs (Gemini / DeepSeek) to automatically score resumes across multiple dimensions, helping HR quickly identify top candidates and improve screening efficiency by over 80%.

▸ 操作指南How to Use

1

设置职位描述 (JD)Set Up Job Description

  • 在左侧面板粘贴 JD 文本，或点击右下角按钮上传 JD 文件（PDF / DOCX / TXT）Paste JD text in the left panel, or click the bottom-right button to upload a JD file (PDF/DOCX/TXT)
  • 点击 Auto-configure from JD 按钮，AI 会自动解析职位名称、建议评分权重、设置硬性门槛Click Auto-configure from JD — AI will extract job title, suggest weights, and set thresholds
  • 手动调整权重滑块（侧边栏可展开）和最低学历/经验要求Manually fine-tune weight sliders (expandable in sidebar) and minimum education/experience
📸 截图：JD 配置面板Screenshot: JD Configuration Panel
💡 提示Tip: JD 越详细，AI 的权重建议越精准。建议至少包含 3-8 个必备技能标签。 The more detailed your JD, the more accurate the AI's weight suggestions. Include at least 3-8 required skills.
2

上传简历Upload Resumes

  • 在右侧面板的虚线区域点击或拖拽上传简历（支持 PDF / DOCX / TXT，单次最多 50 份）Click or drag files into the dashed upload zone (PDF/DOCX/TXT, max 50 files)
  • 已上传的文件会以卡片形式显示在下方，可单独移除或一键清空Uploaded files appear as cards — remove individually or clear all
📸 截图：简历上传区域Screenshot: Resume Upload Zone
3

开始筛选Start Screening

  • 点击底部黄色的 Screen Candidates → 按钮Click the yellow Screen Candidates → button at the bottom
  • 系统将并发分析所有简历，顶部进度条显示处理进度The system processes all resumes concurrently — progress bar shows status
  • 每份简历约需 3-10 秒，请保持页面打开Each resume takes ~3-10 seconds — keep the browser tab active
⚡ 性能说明Performance Note: 系统以 4 个并发同时处理简历。50 份简历预计耗时 1-2 分钟。 The system processes 4 resumes in parallel. 50 resumes take approximately 1-2 minutes.
4

查看结果Review Results

  • 筛选完成后自动跳转到结果页，候选人按分数降序排列Automatically navigates to Results page — candidates sorted by score (highest first)
  • 点击任意候选人卡片展开详情：各维度柱状图、核心亮点、不足与风险、AI 推荐意见Click any card to expand: score chart, highlights, gaps, AI recommendation
  • 底部面板显示简历原文 PDF 预览Bottom panel shows original resume PDF preview
  • 使用顶部过滤器按分数段或匹配状态筛选Use filters to narrow by score range or match status
📸 截图：筛选结果页面Screenshot: Results Page
5

人工复核与调分HR Review & Score Override

  • 展开候选人卡片，滚动到底部 Adjust Score 区域Expand a candidate card and scroll to the Adjust Score section
  • 输入调整后的分数和原因（如"经验不足"、"资历过深"），点击保存Enter adjusted score and reason (e.g. "Under-qualified", "Over-qualified"), then save
  • 调整后的分数会标记为 VERIFIED，覆盖 AI 原始评分Overridden scores are marked VERIFIED and supersede AI scores
6

使用人才库Using the Talent Library

  • 点击侧边栏 Talent Library 查看所有历史筛选候选人Click Talent Library in the sidebar to browse all screened candidates across jobs
  • 支持按姓名、技能搜索，按分数段过滤（Strong / Pending / Rejected）Search by name or skill; filter by score tier (Strong / Pending / Rejected)
  • 点击候选人可快速跳转到对应结果详情页Click any candidate to jump to their detail view

▸ 常见问题FAQ

Q: 支持哪些文件格式？Q: What file formats are supported?
A: PDF、DOCX、DOC 和 TXT 文件。推荐使用 PDF 格式以获得最佳解析效果。A: PDF, DOCX, DOC, and TXT. PDF is recommended for best parsing results.
Q: AI 评分的准确度如何？Q: How accurate is the AI scoring?
A: AI 评分作为初筛参考，建议 HR 对 Top 候选人进行人工复核。系统支持 HR 手动调分并记录理由。A: AI scoring serves as an initial screening reference. HR review of top candidates is recommended. The system supports manual score override with documented reasons.
Q: 我的数据安全吗？Q: Is my data secure?
A: 所有数据存储在 Google Cloud Firestore（asia-east1 区域），AI 调用通过服务端代理，API Key 不会暴露到浏览器。A: All data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore (asia-east1 region). AI calls are proxied through the server — API keys never reach the browser.
Q: 如何切换中英文？Q: How do I switch between Chinese and English?
A: 侧边栏顶部 ST Logo 右侧有语言切换按钮（中文 / EN）。AI 分析结果的语言也会同步切换。A: Use the language toggle button next to the ST logo in the sidebar (中文 / EN). AI analysis results will also switch language.
Q: 权重必须设为 100% 吗？Q: Must weights sum to 100%?
A: 是的。侧边栏会实时显示当前总和，不满足 100% 时会红色警告。A: Yes. The sidebar shows real-time total — a red warning appears if not 100%.
